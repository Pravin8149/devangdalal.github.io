About Devang Dalal Associates

Our Vision

At Devang Dalal MEP Consulting Engineer, we are a team of dynamic professionals offering end-to-end consultancy in HVAC, Electrical, Fire Fighting, Low Voltage Systems & IBMS. Our vision is to deliver safe, sustainable, and cost-effective engineering solutions tailored to modern commercial infrastructure — from IT Parks and Malls to High-Rise Residences and Data Centers.

About Mr. Devang Dalal

Mr. Devang Dalal is a Mechanical Engineer from Mumbai with over 20 years of experience as Sr. Associate with reputed MEP consultancy firms. He has worked with Mr. Rumi H. Bharucha Consulting Engineers, contributing to design and execution of Electrical, HVAC, Plumbing and Fire Fighting Systems.

He has managed numerous LEED-certified green building projects with Gold/Platinum ratings from USGBC. He is also an active member of ISHRAE & ASHRAE. With dedication to quality, energy-efficiency, and modern design thinking, he founded Devang Dalal MEP Consulting Engineer — a multidisciplinary firm providing engineering excellence to a diverse client base.
